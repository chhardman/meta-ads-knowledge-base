## Cost Benchmarks

|-----------|-----|

## Attribution & Tracking

### View-Through vs Click-Through
| Attribution Window | Typical Distribution |
|--------------------|---------------------|
| 1-day view | 20-40% of conversions |
| 7-day click | 40-60% of conversions |
| 28-day click | 10-20% (additional) |

### iOS Impact
- Expect 15-30% underreporting in Meta
- Use blended CAC/ROAS as source of truth
- First-party data increasingly valuable
