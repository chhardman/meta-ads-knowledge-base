# Ecommerce Marketing Calendar 2026

> Plan ahead. Brands planning BFCM need 90-120 days of warm-up. Start early.

---

## Annual Overview

| Quarter | Key Events | CPM Trend | Planning Priority |
|---------|------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Q1 | New Year, Valentine's | Low (-20-30%) | Build audiences, test creative |
| Q2 | Mother's Day, Memorial Day | Baseline | Scale winners, prep for Q3 |
| Q3 | Back to School, Labor Day | +10-20% | Heavy testing, Q4 prep |
| Q4 | BFCM, Holidays | +40-80% | Scale, maximize |

**Budget Rule:** If Q4 drives 40% of annual sales, give it 40% of yearly promo spend.

## Q4 2026 (October - December)

### October
**Theme:** Halloween, early holiday shopping

| Date | Event | Campaign Ideas |
|------|-------|----------------|
| Early Oct | Prime Big Deal Days (estimate) | Early holiday competing |
| Oct 31 | Halloween | Costume, party, themed products |

**Key Stats:**
- 41% of shoppers start holiday shopping in October or earlier
- Start warming up audiences NOW
- Test holiday creative
- Early-bird promotions work

---

### November
**Theme:** BFCM, Thanksgiving, peak shopping

| Date | Event | Campaign Ideas |
|------|-------|----------------|
| Nov 11 | Veterans Day | Appreciation sales |
| Nov 26 | Thanksgiving | Gratitude messaging |
| Nov 27 | Black Friday | PEAK |
| Nov 28-30 | BFCM Weekend | Maximum push |
| Nov 30 | Cyber Monday | Online focus |

**BFCM Strategy:**
- CPMs +40-80% above baseline
- Have creative ready by early October
- Warm audiences for 90-120 days
- Daily creative refresh during peak
- Real-time budget optimization

**BFCM Stats:**
- +103% traffic vs. normal
- +79% conversion rate increase
- Only 22% repurchase within 12 months (focus on retention)

---

### December
**Theme:** Holiday gifts, year-end

| Date | Event | Campaign Ideas |
|------|-------|----------------|
| Dec 1-15 | Gift Guide Season | Shipping deadlines focus |
| Dec 15-20 | Last Shipping Days | Urgency messaging |
| Dec 21-24 | Last Minute | Digital gifts, gift cards |
| Dec 26-31 | Post-Holiday | Clearance, New Year prep |

**December Phases:**
1. **Early Dec (1-10):** Gift guide heavy, full shipping available
2. **Mid Dec (11-18):** Shipping deadline urgency
3. **Late Dec (19-24):** Digital/gift cards only
4. **Post-Holiday (26-31):** Clearance + New Year transition
