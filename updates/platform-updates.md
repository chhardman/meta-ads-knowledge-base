## December 2025 Updates

### AI Personalization Change (December 16, 2025)
**Impact: HIGH**

Meta now uses your interactions with AI at Meta to personalize content and ads, including posts and reels. This affects how ads are served and targeted.

**What it means for advertisers:**
- AI signals are now factored into ad delivery
- Content engagement patterns influence ad targeting
- More emphasis on creative quality for AI matching

---

### Advantage+ Now Default
**Impact: HIGH**

Meta updated Ads Manager to make Advantage+ automation tools the **default option** for new campaigns.

**Action items:**
- [ ] Audit existing manual campaigns
- [ ] Test Advantage+ vs manual side-by-side
- [ ] Ensure creative library is diverse enough for AI optimization

---

### Opportunity Score Feature
**Impact: MEDIUM**

New 0-100 opportunity score evaluates campaign optimization based on 40+ personalized recommendations.

**How to use:**
- Access via Marketing API or Ads Manager
- Score indicates optimization potential
- Higher score = more recommendations implemented

---

### Lead Generation Enhancements
**Impact: HIGH for Lead Gen**

- Advantage+ Leads Campaigns now **globally available**
- New automated Messenger follow-up feature
- Website + Instant Forms combination now supported
- Two new verification tools: SMS and Work Email verification

**Action items:**
- [ ] Test Advantage+ Leads if running lead gen
- [ ] Enable Messenger auto-engagement
- [ ] Add verification for lead quality

---

### Partnership Ads Improvements
**Impact: MEDIUM**

- Better insights dashboard
- Expanded Partnership Ads Hub
- New Partnership Ads API
- Simplified creator permissions
- Creator eligibility now includes professional mode profiles

**Stats:** Partnership ads reduce costs and boost engagement. AI creator testimonials enhance performance.

---

### Value Rules Expansion
**Impact: MEDIUM**

New "Conversion location" option for value rules. You can now set custom values for:
- Website conversions
- App conversions
- Instant Forms conversions

**Use case:** Increase bids for Instant Form conversions if they convert better downstream.

---

### Dynamic Creative Enhancements
**Impact: HIGH**

Meta's AI now:
- Highlights key selling points as overlays
- Auto-highlights important points in longer videos
- Can display positive reviews directly on ads (if page allows reviews)

**Action items:**
- [ ] Upload multiple captions/media per ad
- [ ] Enable reviews on Facebook page
- [ ] Test longer video with auto-highlights

---

### Creator Management Tools Discontinued
**Impact: LOW-MEDIUM**

Creator Management Tools discontinued December 1, 2025. Use Partnership Ads Hub instead.

## 2025 Major Changes Summary

### The Andromeda Algorithm (Q1 2025)
**Impact: CRITICAL**

Complete overhaul of ad delivery system:
- 10,000x larger ML models than before
- Creative is now the primary targeting signal
- Broad targeting outperforms narrow
- Simple campaign structures work best

**What broke:**
- Narrow interest targeting
- Lookalike audiences
- Complex campaign structures
- Minor creative variations

**What works:**
- Broad audiences (10M+)
- 8-15 genuinely different concepts
- Simple structure (1 campaign, 1 ad set)
- Advantage+ automation

---

### Cost Increases (Throughout 2025)
- CPMs up 18%+ YoY in Q1
- Average CPM hit $12.74 by June
- CPL increased 21% to $27.66

---

### Advantage+ Expansion
- Usage grew 70% YoY
- 9% lower CPA on average
- 14% lower cost per qualified lead vs manual
- Now default for new campaigns

---

### Creative Testing Feature (Mid-2025)
Built-in A/B testing that:
- Prevents optimization to specific ads
- Distributes spend evenly
- Shows each user only one variant
- Calculates statistical significance

**Requirements:**
- Highest Volume bid strategy
- Daily budget (no lifetime)
- Up to 5 creatives per test
- 20% max budget recommended
