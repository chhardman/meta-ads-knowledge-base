## Quarterly Competitor Review

### Questions to Answer

1. **Creative Evolution**
   - How has their creative style changed?
   - What formats are they investing more in?
   - What have they stopped doing?

2. **Messaging Shifts**
   - What pain points are they targeting?
   - How are they positioning vs. us?
   - Any new angles emerging?

3. **Offer Strategy**
   - What promotions are they running?
   - How aggressive is their discounting?
   - Any new offer structures?

4. **Volume & Investment**
   - More or fewer active ads?
   - Higher or lower estimated spend?
   - Testing more or scaling existing?

## Competitive Response Playbook

### If Competitor Launches New Campaign
1. Screenshot and log immediately
2. Track for 7 days (is it scaling?)
3. If scaling, analyze deeply
4. Develop our response/adaptation within 2 weeks

### If Competitor Copies Our Approach
1. Document the similarity
2. Double down if it validates our concept
3. Iterate to stay ahead
4. Consider new angle to differentiate
