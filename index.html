Video Hook Swipe File

73% of ecommerce video ads fail within the first 3 seconds. These hooks stop the scroll.

Pattern 1: The Comment Skeptic (TikTok Style)

Display a negative comment overlay, then have the creator respond directly.

[Comment: "Those seem so extra"] "Okay I get why you'd think that, but let me show you..."
[Comment: "This is just a gimmick"] "I thought the same thing until..."

Why it works: Negativity psychology drives engagement. Addresses objections head-on.

Pattern 2: People Always Ask Me

Leverage the trending format with natural product integration.

"People always ask me how I [achieve result], so here it is..."
"Everyone keeps asking about my [specific thing], let me show you..."

Why it works: Creates curiosity and social proof simultaneously.

Pattern 3: Direct Problem Callout

State the pain point immediately, then pivot to solution.

"Tired of [problem]? Watch this."
"If you're struggling with [issue], you need to see this."
"Nobody talks about [problem], so I will."

Why it works: Immediate resonance with target audience.

Pattern 4: The Surprising Statement

Open with something unexpected or controversial.

"I was today years old when I found out..."
"This is the [product] nobody's talking about..."
"Your [common item] isn't as good as you think."

Why it works: Pattern interrupt + curiosity gap.

Pattern 5: Before-After Visual

Show dramatic transformation in first 2 seconds.

[Split screen of before/after]
"From this... to THIS" [visual transformation]

Why it works: Visual and impactful, shows proof immediately.

Pattern 6: The FOMO Trigger

Create urgency through scarcity or exclusivity.

"This is flying off the shelves and I finally understand why..."
"Run, don't walk - this won't last..."
"If you see this, it's your sign to..."

Why it works: Fear of missing out drives action.

Headlines & Copy Hooks

Headlines should be 40 characters or less. Pick ONE tactic: urgency, curiosity, benefit, or problem-solution.

Classic Copywriting Formulas

AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)

Attention: Hook with strong headline
Interest: Share relatable pain/problem
Desire: Highlight the transformation
Action: Clear CTA

Finally, skincare that actually works. (Attention)
Tried every product on the shelf? Same. (Interest)
Our customers see visible results in 14 days - or your money back. (Desire)
Shop now and save 20%. (Action)
PAS (Problem, Agitate, Solution)

Problem: State the pain point
Agitate: Twist the knife
Solution: Present the answer

Waking up exhausted every morning? (Problem)
You've tried sleeping more, coffee, energy drinks... nothing works. (Agitate)
Our natural energy formula changed everything for 50,000+ customers. (Solution)
FAB (Feature, Advantage, Benefit)

Feature: What it is/has
Advantage: Why that matters
Benefit: How life improves

Made with 100% organic cotton (Feature)
So it's softer and lasts 3x longer than regular shirts (Advantage)
Which means you'll finally have a go-to tee that feels amazing every time (Benefit)

Hook Categories

Number/Statistic Hooks

  • "93% of customers reorder within 30 days"
  • "5 million sold and counting"
  • "In just 7 days, you'll notice the difference"

Curiosity Hooks

  • "What if I told you..."
  • "The one thing dermatologists don't want you to know"
  • "This changes everything about [topic]"

Fear/FOMO Hooks

  • "While you wait, your competitors are..."
  • "Limited stock - only [X] left"
  • "This deal ends tonight"

Empathy Hooks

  • "We get it. [Problem] is exhausting."
  • "You're not alone in feeling..."
  • "For everyone who's tried everything..."

Testing Frameworks

Systematic testing to find winners - creative accounts for 70% of success

The 3-Phase Testing Funnel

Phase 1: Concept Testing (Week 1-2)

Goal: Find winning creative concepts

  • Budget: 10-20% of total
  • Variations: 8-15 genuinely different concepts
  • Metrics: Hook rate, CTR, CPM
  • Kill criteria: No traction in 24-48 hours
Phase 2: Hook/Element Testing (Week 3-4)

Goal: Optimize winning concepts

  • Budget: 15% of total
  • Variations: 3-5 per winning concept
  • Metrics: Hook rate, watch time, CTR
  • Test: Different hooks, CTAs, thumbnails
Phase 3: Scale Testing (Ongoing)

Goal: Find scale potential

  • Platform: Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns
  • Metrics: CPA, ROAS, scale ceiling
  • Action: Move winners to main campaigns
